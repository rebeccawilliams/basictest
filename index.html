DC Legal Hackers

Improving law with technology and technology with law.

Technology bustles ahead, but our legal tools and tech related policy hardly reflect this. What gives? This organization challenges Legal Hackers to catch legal tools and policy up with technology. Join us in monthly meetups and quarterly hackathons as we tackle these tough problems and create tangible solutions, through: discussions, speakers, demos, lessons (yes, coding), and quarterly hackathons. Our discussions will be aimed at creating proposed resolutions on the spot, so bring your brains and your portable computing devices!
